Medication was not helping much. I needed some explanation and that's when I had one session with Yamini maam and I was blown away. She gave me insight into my past, helped me heal the childhood that was somewhere sitting inside me and not letting me live my life freely.

I have learnt something big in my sessions and that is that life needs to be lived to the fullest and for ourselves.
